About Us:

At VOW Jiu-Jitsu we celebrate the art of Jiu-Jitsu and how it changes a person. We want our students to enjoy their time training – learning that their growth is limitless in an open atmosphere. Despite your age, fitness level, or physical limitations, we truly believe Jiu-Jitsu is for everyone. Our goal is to create a community where we all evolve together and surpass our students expectations and reveal their full potential through the art of Jiu-Jitsu.

Our History:

Jiu-Jitsu originated in Japan and became popular in South America where the Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu we know today was born. It started as a martial art rooted in peace and self-defense, but has grown popular in the competitive space and for its health benefits. The goal of BJJ has remained the same through the years – take down your opponent, get around their legs, and apply a submission. This is achieved through technique, timing, and control.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu continues to grow rapidly and spread through different cultures and academies. VOW Jiu-Jitsu has played a part in its growth since Summer 2018, when our academy was first established in Austin, Texas. We continuously strive to educate students with different teaching methods regardless of their learning style or experience level. Our methods have created a team of strong martial artists like never before.

Why It's Important

Passion

VOW Jiu-Jitsu shows you what you are capable of and celebrates your goals. Being surrounded by like-minded martial artists will ignite a spark within.

Perseverance

Jiu-Jitsu teaches you how to overcome obstacles and how to learn from adversity. It may be hard work, but you’ll learn to apply yourself in work, school, and life.

Teamwork

Your teammates and instructors want you to grow and succeed. They’ll encourage you while educating you.

Growth

BJJ classes not only make you stronger physically, but mentally too. Training brings forth mental fortitude and teaches you life lessons.

Confidence

Consistently testing your strength while learning how to be better will improve your confidence. You learn about yourself in a new way and take on the world differently after BJJ training.
