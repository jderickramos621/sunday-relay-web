Handoff

When a human
needs to step in.

Some questions need a person. When the agent isn't confident, it doesn't guess — it takes a clean message and gets it to the right hands.

Collects contact info

Name, number or email, and a short note on what they need — so no one starts cold.

Routes to the right person

Pricing goes to the owner. Scheduling goes to the front desk. You decide the routing once.

Escalates nuance

Medical questions, refunds, anything sensitive — flagged for a human, never improvised.

Requests follow-up

Sets the expectation (“someone will reach out by tomorrow”) and logs it so it doesn't slip.

The goal isn't to replace your staff — it's to protect their time.
